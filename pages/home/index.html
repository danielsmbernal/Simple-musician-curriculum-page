Profile

Daniel Bernal started playing guitar at the age of fourteen. It didn't take long for his passion for music to lead him to formalize his studies, graduating with a bachelor's degree in guitar and as an audio technician.

In addition to his performance as an instrumentalist in bands of different styles, Daniel's main activities are teaching and recording.

The curiosity and demand of students for other instruments led him to learn, in a self-taught way, to sing and play other instruments.
